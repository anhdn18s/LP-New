Among Us - The Best Mobile Game ever created

Murder mystery games like Mafia or Werewolves are legendary, but Among Us has taken this genre to a new level. Instead of having to win people’s trust over arguments, players can silently become an undercover impostor in the Among Us universe and focus only on how to kill everyone else. Others will try to guess which one is the real impostor when a dead body is found. The game only ends when Crewmates can identify and eject all the impostors. This format presents a very fast and engaging gameplay, with each game lasting only 10 to 15 minutes, yet still able to hook everyone into the flow of the game. No wonder why during the pandemic, this game went viral all over the world.

Among Us can either take place on a spaceship in outer space, a building high in the Earth’s atmosphere, an alien planet or an airship. There could be up to 3 impostors in a total of 15 crew members of the game, which makes it tough for the crewmates to identify the murderers. At the beginning, crewmates are assigned “tasks” to complete around the map, which mostly consist of maintenance work like fixing wires or data downloading. Besides ejecting all impostors, completing all assigned tasks could also help crewmates win. The impostors will pretend that they’re performing tasks, but what they were really doing was trying to sabotage everything and kill the crewmates whenever possible.

There’s also a discussion part in this game where any player can call a group meeting by reporting a dead body, or by pressing the Emergency Meeting button on the map at any time. During the meeting, everyone discusses with each other and votes on who the impostors are. People can skip their votes if they’re unsure and if there’s a tie in the voting result then the chosen person will not be eliminated. The goal of the impostors is to not be eliminated by everyone else, but they can sacrifice their teammates if the action can earn everyone’s trust. When a player is eliminated, the game will notify everyone whether they’ve picked the right impostor.

It was until 2020 that Among Us saw a surge of popularity all over the world. The Covid-19 pandemic was credited as the reason for the game’s growth, since Among Us allows everyone to socialize during social distancing. After Covid, Among Us had almost 20 billion views on Youtube and Tiktok, making it one of the most popular mobile games in history.
