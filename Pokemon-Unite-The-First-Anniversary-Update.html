Pokemon Unite: The First Anniversary Update

September, 2022, marks the first anniversary of the 2021 Google Play’s best game Pokémon UNITE. TiMi studio group has announced a big major change within the game to mark this special occasion.

There have already been over five million pre-registrations for the Mobile Version. These early fans will definitely receive Aeos tickets, Unite License: Pikachu, and Festival Style: Pikachu as gifts. And these gifts are only the beginning, as the update presents so much more.

Battle Pass 10

Season 10 battle pass is now available. Battle pass is an in-game feature that rewards you with items after completing quests/missions. Season 10 battle pass will offer new rewards such as fashion items for Trainer, new Holowear like Wanderer Style: Azumarill or even rewards from the Season 1 and Season 2 battle passes such as Captain Style: Cinderace, Space Style: Gengar,...

Mew’s Mural Challenge and Two Newly Added Pokémon

In addition, you can expect a new feature which is the Mythical Pokémon Mew. You can participate in the Mew’s Mural Challenge event and obtain Mew’s Unite license without spending Aeos coins/gems (an in-game currency).

You will need to win battles, get quests done to collect all the required six mural fragments. Mew’s Unite license can only be obtained at the Unite Battle Committee shop for 12,000 Aeos coins at the end the event, but you will be able to use Aeos gems to progress through Mew’s Mural Challenge faster.

With Mew’s debut, two more Mons will be added: Dodrio and Scizor. Also, the Legendary giants Regieleki, Regirock, Regice, and Registeel will make their debut as wild Pokémon. The rewards for defeating Regirock, Regice, and Registeel will vary.

Besides, current pokemons and wild pokemons will have new or redefined features. For example: Wild Pokémon Regigigas will grant speed increase and unblockable goals after your team defeats it. Charizard’s Fire Blast will cool down, and Eldegoss’s Leaf Tornado will improve to match the feel of the controls.

New Bosses, New Battle Ground, Legacy Trainer Showdown

When a Unite Battle begins, Leon, Raihan, Cynthia, Korrina, and Green will try to take you down. If you defeat the legacy trainer by using the daily Trainer battle ticket given by the game, you’ll obtain trainer coins to exchange for items. You can only receive one per day but you can exchange Aeos coins for additional tickets.

Ranked matches and standard battles will take place in the Theia Sky Ruins above Aeos Island. In the final two minutes of the game, the Sky High Pokémon Rayquaza will appear in the central area. If your team defeats Rayquaza, you will receive a temporary shield with special effects. While the shield is active, attack power will be increased and scoring attempts will be unblockable.

Gift: Unite Licenses and Holowear

During the event, players will be able to get Unite licenses and Holowear by logging in for up to five days (no need to be consecutive days), with the rewards being, Pikachu, Lucario, Blastoise, Snorlax, Sylveon and their Holowear styles.
