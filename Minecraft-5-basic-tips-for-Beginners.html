Minecraft: 5 Basic Tips For Beginners

Minecraft is one of the most successful and popular games of all time. Developed by Mojang, Minecraft already has more than 200 million copies sold and 126 million monthly active users. There are some basic survival tips you absolutely need to become a better player in Minecraft. Here are the five basic tips.

1.Punch A Tree!

When you first start out, you have nothing: no equipment, no weapons, no housing. Therefore, getting some tools is the first thing you should do. To accomplish this, approach the closest tree and start punching it until the wood begins to fall. You'll be using wood as your primary building material throughout the game. Gather several logs, then access your inventory to convert them into planks. After that you should use four planks to create a Crafting Table in your mini crafting grid. Crafting Table is a useful item as it will allow you to create tools, items, weapons,...

2.Building A Basic Tool

With the help of the Crafting Table, you can build yourself a wooden pickaxe by crafting sticks (created from planks) with wood planks. Once you have the wooden pickaxe, use it to gather stones and then craft stone versions of pickaxes, axes, hoes, shovels, and swords. With these new items, you can protect yourselves from dangerous creatures, mine more materials and hunt/gather for food.

3.Understand Your Hunger

When you walk, sprint or jump, you will lose your energy. Your hunger meter is shown on the bottom right of your screen and represented by chunks of meat. As your hunger meter begins to decrease, you will lose your abilities such as regenerating health or sprinting. You might even starve to death. To regain them, you have to consume food. Gain basic foodstuffs like meat from animals and fruits from trees to keep you alive. Some special food might also provide more points for your hunger meter than others, with cooked beef and cooked salmon as primary examples.

4.Survive Your First Nights

When the sun goes down, zombies, creepers or skeletons will come and attack. Therefore, it is essential to build a shelter to protect you from harm. You can build a basic shelter by using the wood blocks from trees, then create some torches by crafting sticks with mined coals. These torches will further protect you from your enemies since they are all afraid of the light from the fire.

5.Explore

So what would you do after surviving the night? Go outside in the morning and explore the universe. Exploring will give you a chance to find new materials to upgrade your items or shelters. You might even meet the villagers, a really important factor during the next phase of your adventure. What you do from here is totally up to your imagination, and this is why the game is legendary. There are dozens of dimensions to explore, various enemies to fight, unlimited resources for you to build everything such as your own minecraft version of a garden, your hometown or even Hogwarts. Take a moment to plan your next steps and have fun exploring this wonderful game.

By visiting classic.minecraft.net, you can play Minecraft without having to download or install anything. A new version of the game Minecraft Legends, will be set to be released on all platforms in the first half of 2023.
