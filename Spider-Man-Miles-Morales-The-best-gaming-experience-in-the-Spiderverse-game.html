Spider-Man: Miles Morales - The best gaming experience in the Spiderverse game

The spin-off version and the sequel of the Marvel's Spider-Man (2018), Spider-Man: Miles Morales has been ranked with high reception amongst the gaming community and platforms, scoring 9/10 at IGN and 4.5/5 at Gamestop. With a Windows version set to be released in late 2022 and Marvel's Spider-Man 2 featuring both Peter Parker and Miles Morales as main protagonists coming soon in 2023, here are some reasons why Spider-Man: Miles Morales is more than just a spin-off.

He’s not Peter Parker

Miles holds great pride as a mixed-race hero from African American and Puerto Rican heritage. Miles is a hero who has a family, a very different factor from Peter Parker who only has Aunt May. Miles’s Mom is running for the city council and she has a big impact on the storyline.

You will be surprised to learn that unlike the original Peter Parker, Miles Morales has Bio-electricity, ability right into the game's opening. This new combat style really stands out in the game and offers players new ways to take down the enemies.

Cool and Slick Movement!

Winning the 48th Annie Awards for Outstanding Achievement for Character Animation in a Video, the game presents a very fluid movement. Spider-Man’s parkour is now enhanced, making it the best parkour ever existed.

Players can now perform a series of somersaults while soaring through the sky. The image of Miles placing both arms behind his head, crossing his legs and pretending to lie down after jumping off from a tall skyscraper will soon become iconic.

Interesting Villains

Though having fewer villains than the other Marvel’s Spider-Man Games, Spider-Man: Miles Morales gives the story arc of the villains a more interesting quality. Villains will have a backstory that the player can sympathize with.

Villains will have different motives for their own personal reason or as a group, players will get to know more of it the more they go deeper into the game.

Cool Side Quest, Easter Eggs and Add-ons
Advertisement

After completing the main quest, players can continue on playing the game by doing the side quests provided by an in-game app called “FNSM” created by Miles’ best friend Ganke. Completing these side quests will give the players bonus add-ons such as new costumes, new companions like the Spider Cat. Moreover, while you are roaming New York spitting webs or just running around, you can find different easter eggs, for example the Statue of the legendary Stan Lee, who passed away in 2019.

Though being thought only as a spin-off game, Spider-Man: Miles Morales stands out on its own. This is a must if you are fans of the Spider-Man game franchise. Unlike Peter Parker’s perspective, you are Miles Morales, who has to balance between fighting the bad guys and facing the harsh reality of having a responsibility to keep Harlem safe as a teenage kid. Get ready!
