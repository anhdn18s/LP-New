Spiderman: Miles Morales brings a fresh experience to Spiderman fans all over the world.

Since Peter Parker is already familiar to everyone as the man behind the mask, the introduction of Miles Morales offers a brand new representation of our favorite hero. In the game, Miles has been trained by Peter and is now located in Harlem, a unique area of New York City. He’s in the process of finding his true identity, and as Peter’s leaving New York for a while to support Mary Jane’s work vacation overseas, this is Miles’s perfect opportunity to embrace his role as spiderman.

Advertisement

In the game, Miles Morales investigates the conflict between the company Roxxon Plaza and an organization called “The Underground”, a conflict that could damage New York City. Roxxon Plaza CEO Simon Krieger has created many questionable experiments using the company’s resources, and he also possesses a dangerous villain under his wing - The Rhino. Roxxon is a huge company that can do serious damage if they want to, while the Underground also has a powerful leader with special capabilities. This will be a huge test to Miles’s growing identity as Spiderman as he faces tough challenges, similar to Peter in the beginning. Should he reveal his identity to his mother? Is he brave enough to tackle the enemies after knowing who they really are? Is he strong enough to take care of New York City against powerful villains as a 13-year-old kid? The fans will understand and love Miles even more as they go through the game and feel the new spiderman story arc.

Miles Morales possesses a wonderful range of skills as spiderman, and the game carefully instructs players on how to best utilize these skills. There are guidelines on the screen dedicated to teach gamers how to shoot the web in hundreds of different combinations, or how to throw the new power moves such as the venom punch properly. When Miles encounters crucial situations against enemies, there are also special instructions to help navigate Miles’s fighting strategy. The game can also be seen as a beautiful movie that all Spiderman fans can cheer for. The quality of music and graphics is impeccable, describing New York City in the most authentic way that you can imagine. Gamers can fly around New York City having conversations with the locals, enjoying beautiful Spiderman graffities, and can even enter many famous NYC local dining houses just to see things around. Insomniac has really given us one of the best representations ever of Spiderman in this version.

Players will go through the storyline to understand Miles Morales as a Spiderman character, and have a chance to actually practice his special abilities. Swinging around New York, trying the new spider suit, obtaining new skills, everything will be as lively as ever in this amazing game. Ladies and gentlemen, the new spiderman era is here. The era of Miles Morales.
