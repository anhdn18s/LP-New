Pokemon Unite: 5 tips to make you play better

Pokemon Unite is a new MOBA game taking place in the Pokemon universe. The game has been a mega hit within the gaming community with over 70 million downloads and even won the award for Best Game of Google Play in 2021. In this game, players will take control of a pokemon to compete in a 5v5 battle, and the team with the most points scored wins. Here are 5 basic tips to make you a better player.

1.Control

Choosing the right control will help you a lot during the battles of the game. One feature is turning the enemy lock on in the game settings. This feature will not only help target only one enemy to focus on fighting the main target during combat, but also avoid being disturbed by miscontrols.

2.Knowing your positions

This is a 5v5 game, which means that you will have to work well with your teammates. Picking the right position will give you the chance to play the right position the team needs. In Pokemon Unite, there are five specific roles:

  • Attackers: dealing heavy damage. Attackers have low health and a long cooldown period on their moves. (For example: Pikachu, Greninja, Venusaur)
  • Defenders: Tanky pokemons that are able to protect their teammates. They work best when paired with Attackers in the Top or Bottom lane. Defenders are also good at controlling crowds by immobilizing enemies, but are slow to level up and retreat. (For example: Snorlax, Crustle and Slowbro)
  • Speedster: Speedster often takes the Central path, has high mobility and often acts as the team's main scorer. Being located in the central path offers Speedster great position to rush to support either Top or Bottom lane. Like Attackers, they have low durability. (For example: Talonflame, Absol, Zeraora, and Gengar)
  • Supporters: one of the key Battle Types to have. Supporters can assist teammates by providing team healing and giving negative status conditions on enemies. A Supporter works best when paired with an Attacker or an All-Rounder in the Top or Bottom lane since they do not have high HP. (For example: Wigglytuff, Eldegoss or Mr. Mime)
  • All Rounders: If you still don’t know which role suits you best, an All-Rounder might be the way to go. A hybrid between Attackers and Defenders, All Rounders are useful in many situations. All-Rounders can not be a Supporter role since they don’t provide healing ability. (For example: Charizard, Lucario, Machamp and Garchomp)
3.Ulitize Game Resources

The in-game features of Pokemon Unite will give you a strategic edge if they are used wisely. Using the teleportation and jump pads will save you the time from moving from one place to another. Hiding in tall grass which shields players from sight can provide an opportunity to set an ambush. Upgrading items after a match will give you a huge advantage for the next game. And finally, pay attention to the mini-map as all the major actions will be reflected in there.

4.Bosses

Defeating bosses will give you bonus buffs that will help in battles. You should know when the bosses appear on the map so that your team can make the right strategies. Pay attention during the boss fight as the enemies can steal your kill and destroy your team just by waiting for the HP to go down.

5.Sacrifice Goals

Like other MOBAs, it’s sometimes better to let your enemies have one of your goals rather than try to defend it. Always try to defend the goal when you are at full HP and have your teammates nearby. If things do not go well, abandon that goal and go for another plan. Losing one goal does not mean your team will lose the whole match.

These few tips will surely make you a better player of the game. Just like many MOBAs before, Pokemon Unite requires players to have a good understanding of the game mechanics & the Pokemon they are playing. Leading your favorite Pokemon to battle in this game is a unique experience, and Pokemon Unite deserves all the accolades it’s having in the community.

Pokemon Unite is playable on Android and iOS devices in addition to the Nintendo Switch.
