Pokemon Unite introduces the most interesting multiplayer format ever for a Pokemon game.

All fans of Pokemon know the feeling of running around the streets to find hidden Pokemon and battle with strangers from Pokemon Go. They also are too familiar with the storylines evident in many versions of the Pokemon game in history. There’s demand for a new format where their Pokemons are used in battle, where there are many competitive players, and where the Pokemons are still grown beautifully through a process just like in the storytelling games. In other words, Pokemon Unite is the game that all Pokemon fans are waiting for.

Pokemon Unite runs as a multiplayer online battle arena game. There are two teams competing with each other, with each team having 5 players and also, 5 pokemons. Each pokemon will be weak at the beginning, and the players will be in charge of growing their own pokemons through defeating obstacles in the map. The more the Pokemon grows, the more powerful it becomes. These pokemons then can be used as weapons to defeat other team’s pokemons, and to accomplish specific goals to win the game. The game is limited to 10 minutes duration, and the team that achieves the most goals will be the winner with the highest scores.

Originally, there were 20 Pokemons available for players to use. But now as the audience pool grows over time, the number of Pokemons available has increased to 36. Legendary pokemons such as Pikachu, Charizard, Blastoise, Snorlax, Mew are all present. New faces like Aegilash, Lucario, Absol, Garchomp are included to bring more variety and excitement to the fans. Each and every Pokemon possesses unique abilities, which makes linking them together as a team a very engaging task for the game players.

Advertisement

Pokemon Unite receives a very promising welcome from the gaming community even though the game was just launched not too long ago. The game received the Google Play Best Mobile Game of 2021 Award, a very prestigious award. The game’s also officially recognized as a part of the Pokemon World Championship taking place in August 2022, joining other well-known Pokemon games such as the TCG, Pokemon Go and Pokken Tournament.

The game Pokemon Unite has officially passed the 70 million downloads mark in April 2022, and the numbers are still growing strong everyday. The gameplay design is definitely top-notch quality, and the battles are so good that players consider them the best competitive Pokemon game ever made. Players want a change in Pokemon games, and Pokemon Unite is the answer.
