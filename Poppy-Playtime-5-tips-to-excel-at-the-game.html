Poppy Playtime: 5 tips to excel at the game

Even though Poppy Playtime is a newcomer in the horror game genres, it’s already taking the game fans by storm. With a mysterious gameplay, a suspenseful atmosphere, and terrific villains, Poppy Playtime’s cementing itself as the new phenomenon of the horror genre. Here are five tips to help you play the game better.

Advertisement

1.Search Everywhere

One of the reasons why Poppy Playtime stands out among its peers is that there’s no instructions in the game. Players will have to figure out the game by themselves. Every corner of the factory, from the gift shop to the power generator, could contain crucial information. The code to unlock the security door, the key to turn on the electricity, the machine handles lying in the corner, every little thing. These could all be valuable solutions to survive this frightening factory. Keep an eye on everything around!

Advertisement

2.Take time

In the game, the players are free to roam around the factory. The game will only progress if you figure out the right action or the right destination. It’s an open game, so time isn’t something that players should worry about. Take your time to find the right path or the right objects. Use your time to examine every little object and determine if it’s helpful. Don’t rush it, as danger might catch you sooner if you’re not careful!

3.Take advantage of the GrabPack

GrabPack is a very important in-game feature. You might think that the GrabPack is only used to solve puzzles or grab random objects, but it’s so much more than that. GrabPack can be used to open doors, clear out boxes to find important objects hidden behind or pull down the machine handles. These will become very helpful in situations where you need to solve the puzzles creatively and tackle the villains.

Advertisement

4.Look For Video Tapes

Presented as "instructional" videos, the video tapes scattered throughout the factory are special clues to understand the mysteries behind the factory. Playing these tapes will not only reveal hidden details, but also provide special tips and tricks to play the game. A video of a key character before turning into a monster, a private discussion of the Playtime Co.’s staff, or a guide on the devices’ uses are the primary examples.. Don’t miss out on these tapes, since they are there for a reason.

Advertisement

5.Don’t be afraid of death

We all want to play the game as smoothly as we can, but trials and errors are unavoidable. Playing a horror game means you have to witness your dead cutscenes a few times before figuring out the right solution. So don’t punish yourself too hard when Huggy Wuggy catches you or when you fail Mommy Long Legs’ puzzle games in Poppy Playtime. Remember it’s a game. Have fun, try it again, pay attention to clues and objects at the factory, you will find the answer to move on. Death is a part of the game.

Poppy Playtime is now available on both the PC version and the mobile version. With Chapter I and II having been a hit, fans of the game are waiting for the third installment which will be released at the end of 2022 or early 2023.
